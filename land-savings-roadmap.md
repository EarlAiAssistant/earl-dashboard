## Financial Requirements

### Down Payment (Critical)
Land loans require **30-40% down payment** (banks view raw land as higher risk than developed property).

- **Minimum down payment:** $36,000 (30% of $120k)
- **Recommended target:** $45,000-$57,000 (35-40%)
- **Why more is better:** Lower monthly payment, better interest rate, stronger negotiating position

### Total Purchase Breakdown (Example: $135k property)
```
Purchase Price:        $135,000
Down Payment (35%):     $47,250
Loan Amount:            $87,750
Estimated Rate:         7-9% APR (land loans higher than mortgages)
Term:                   15-20 years
Monthly Payment:        ~$750-850
```

## Loan Pre-Approval Timeline

### 6 Months Before Target
- Research land loan lenders (local credit unions often best for land)
- Check credit score, clean up any issues
- Gather financial documents (tax returns, pay stubs, bank statements)

### 3 Months Before Target
- Get pre-approved for land loan
- Know your exact budget + monthly payment
- Understand property restrictions (buildable? road access? utilities?)

### Ready to Shop
- With pre-approval in hand, you're a cash-equivalent buyer
- Can move fast when the right property appears
- Negotiate from position of strength

## Property Research Checklist

While saving, research properties to know exactly what you want:

**Must-Haves:**
- [ ] Road access (private road, public road, or seasonal access?)
- [ ] Buildable land (zoning allows residential structures?)
- [ ] Water source (well potential, nearby lake/river?)
- [ ] Power options (grid hookup vs. solar/generator?)
- [ ] Property taxes (annual cost?)
- [ ] Survey availability (boundary lines clear?)

**Nice-to-Haves:**
- [ ] Wooded vs. open land ratio
- [ ] Topography (flat, rolling hills, waterfront?)
- [ ] Wildlife/hunting potential
- [ ] Proximity to town (30 min? 1 hour?)
- [ ] Neighbors (how close?)

## Resources

### Land Search Sites:
- **LandWatch.com** - Best for filtering by acreage, price, features
- **Zillow** - Good for listings, but less land-specific filters
- **Land and Farm** - Focuses on rural/agricultural properties
- **Craigslist** - Occasional gems, owner-financed deals
- **Local realtors** - Many UP listings don't hit national sites

### Financing Options:
- **Local credit unions** - Often best rates for land loans (check UP-based CUs)
- **Farm Credit Services** - Specializes in rural/ag land
- **Traditional banks** - Higher rates, but more options
- **Owner financing** - Negotiate directly with seller (often better terms)

### Michigan UP Resources:
- **UP Land Conservancy** - Info on land preservation, access rights
- **Michigan DNR** - Public land maps, hunting/fishing regs
- **UP Engineers & Architects** - For buildability assessments
