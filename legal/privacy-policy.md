# Privacy Policy for Call-Content

**Last Updated:** January 31, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, password

- **Content You Upload:** Transcripts, audio/video files, and generated content
- **Communications:** Messages you send us via email or support chat

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide the Service:** Process transcripts, generate content, manage your account
- **Improve the Service:** Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
- **Communicate:** Send transactional emails (receipts, password resets, usage alerts)
- **Marketing:** Send promotional emails (you can opt out anytime)
- **Legal Compliance:** Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

## 3. AI Processing and Your Content

### 3.1 How We Process Your Transcripts
- We use third-party AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic) to process your transcripts
- Your content is sent to these providers to generate blog posts, case studies, etc.
- **We do NOT use your content to train AI models** unless you explicitly opt in

### 3.2 Data Retention
- **Active Content:** Stored as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Content:** Permanently deleted within 30 days of your request
- **Backups:** May be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery

### 3.3 Your Content Ownership
- **You own your content.** We do not claim ownership of transcripts or generated content.
- You grant us a limited license to process your content to provide the Service.
