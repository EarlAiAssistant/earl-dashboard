### Step 2: File Certificate of Organization

**Where:** Idaho Secretary of State  
**URL:** https://sosbiz.idaho.gov/  
**Cost:** $100 (online filing)

**Information Needed:**
| Field | Your Entry |
|-------|------------|
| LLC Name | Voxify LLC |
| Principal Address | Your Sandpoint address |
| Mailing Address | Same (or PO Box if preferred) |
| Registered Agent | Drew Kubacki (yourself) |
| Registered Agent Address | Your Sandpoint address |
| Organizer Name | Drew Kubacki |
| Management | Member-managed (single owner) |

**Process:**
1. Go to https://sosbiz.idaho.gov/
2. Click "Start a New Business"
3. Select "Limited Liability Company"
4. Fill in the form with info above
5. Pay $100 with credit card
6. Save your confirmation number

**Timeline:** Typically processed within 5-10 business days

---

### Step 3: Get an EIN (Tax ID Number)

**Where:** IRS  
**URL:** https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online  
**Cost:** FREE

**When:** After your LLC is approved by Idaho

**Process:**
1. Go to IRS EIN Assistant (link above)
2. Select "Limited Liability Company"
3. Select "Started a new business"
4. Enter LLC info (name, address)
5. Enter your SSN as the responsible party
6. Complete online - **instant EIN assignment**
7. Download and save the confirmation letter

**Hours:** Only available Mon-Fri, 7am-10pm Eastern

---

### Step 4: Open a Business Bank Account

**Recommended:** A simple business checking account

**Good Options:**
- **Mercury** - Online, no fees, great for SaaS (my recommendation)
- **Relay** - Similar to Mercury, also good
- **Chase** - If you prefer a physical branch

**Documents Needed:**
- Certificate of Organization (from Step 2)
- EIN confirmation letter (from Step 3)
- Your driver's license
- Operating Agreement (optional but recommended - see Step 5)

### Step 5: Create an Operating Agreement (Optional but Recommended)

Even as a single-member LLC, an Operating Agreement:
- Strengthens liability protection
- Required by some banks
- Documents ownership/management structure

**Simple Template for Single-Member LLC:**

```
OPERATING AGREEMENT OF VOXIFY LLC

This Operating Agreement is entered into as of [DATE] by Drew Kubacki 
("Member"), as the sole member of Voxify LLC, an Idaho limited 
liability company.

1. FORMATION
The Company was formed by filing a Certificate of Organization with 
the Idaho Secretary of State.

2. NAME AND ADDRESS
Name: Voxify LLC
Address: [Your Sandpoint address]

3. PURPOSE
To engage in any lawful business activity permitted under Idaho law, 
including but not limited to software-as-a-service products.

4. MEMBER
Drew Kubacki - 100% ownership interest

5. MANAGEMENT
The Company shall be member-managed. The Member has full authority 
to make all decisions regarding the Company.

6. CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS
Initial contribution: $[amount, can be $0 or nominal like $100]

7. DISTRIBUTIONS
Net profits and losses shall be allocated to the Member.

8. DISSOLUTION
The Company may be dissolved by written consent of the Member.

Signed: _________________________ Date: _____________
        Drew Kubacki, Member
```

Print, sign, and keep with your business records.

### Step 7: Ongoing Requirements

**Annual Report:**
- Due by end of anniversary month each year
- Cost: $0 (Idaho has no annual report fee for LLCs!)
- Just confirm info is still accurate

**Taxes:**
- LLC income passes through to your personal tax return
- File Schedule C with your 1040
- Pay self-employment tax on net profit (15.3%)
- Make quarterly estimated tax payments if >$1000 expected

## Future: S-Corp Election

**When to consider:** Once Voxify profits exceed ~$40,000/year consistently

**Benefits:**
- Split income into salary + distributions
- Distributions not subject to self-employment tax
- Can save 10-15% on taxes above the threshold

**How:**
- File IRS Form 2553
- Set up payroll for yourself (use Gusto or similar)
- Talk to a CPA first

**Not needed now** - revisit when revenue grows.
