## Quick Tips for Posting

1. **Post timing:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am your local time tends to perform best on LinkedIn
2. **First comment:** Drop a comment with more context or a question to boost engagement
3. **Engage back:** Reply to every comment within the first 2 hours
4. **Tag sparingly:** Only tag people who genuinely would care (don't spam-tag)
