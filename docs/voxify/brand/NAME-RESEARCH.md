## Why Voxify Won

- **Modern & brandable** - Clean, tech-forward sound
- **Premium .com available** - Rare find!
- **Meaning:** "Vox" (Latin for voice) + "-ify" (to make/transform)
- **Conveys:** Voice transformation, action-oriented
- **Short:** 6 letters, easy to type and remember

## Naming Criteria

Based on the brand research, the ideal name should:

1. **Be memorable** - Easy to say, spell, and remember
2. **Convey transformation** - Input (calls) ‚Üí Output (content)
3. **Feel premium** - Not cheap or gimmicky
4. **Be available** - .com domain ideally, or strong alternative TLD
5. **Work globally** - No negative connotations in other languages
6. **Be brandable** - Can build visual identity around it

## Competitor Name Analysis

| Competitor | Name Style | What Works |
|------------|-----------|------------|
| Jasper | Single name, friendly | Personified, memorable |
| Otter | Animal, cute | Very memorable, mascot potential |
| Descript | Descriptive wordplay | Clear what it does |
| Castmagic | Compound + magic | Playful, category + value |

**Pattern:** Most successful names are either single memorable words OR clear compounds.
