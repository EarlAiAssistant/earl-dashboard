## FAQ for Product Hunt Comments

**Q: How is this different from just using ChatGPT?**
> ChatGPT requires prompt engineering for each output type. Voxify is purpose-built for customer transcriptsâ€”one upload, all content types, 60 seconds. No prompting required.

**Q: What AI model do you use?**
> We use Claude (by Anthropic) for content generation. It's excellent at preserving voice and extracting meaningful quotes.

**Q: Is there an API?**
> Not yet, but it's on our roadmap! If you're interested in API access, shoot me an email at drew@getvoxify.com.

**Q: How do you handle privacy?**
> Your transcripts are never used for AI training. Data is encrypted and you can delete it anytime. Full privacy policy on our website.

**Q: What if I don't have transcripts?**
> Most video tools (Zoom, Meet, Teams) auto-generate transcripts. You can also use Otter.ai or Fathom to get transcripts easily.

**Q: Can teams use this?**
> Yes! Professional and Business plans include team features with multiple seats and shared content libraries.
