## Recommended Setup for Voxify

### Option 1: Quick & Free
- **Tool:** Loom (free tier)
- **Pros:** Fast, easy, good quality
- **Cons:** Watermark on free, limited videos
- **Best for:** "See It Work" button, quick demos

### Option 2: Professional Quality (Free)
- **Tool:** OBS Studio + DaVinci Resolve (both free)
- **Pros:** Full control, no watermarks, professional output
- **Cons:** Learning curve, more setup time
- **Best for:** Polished landing page hero video

### Option 3: Interactive Product Tour
- **Tool:** Arcade or Supademo
- **Pros:** Visitors click through themselves, higher engagement
- **Cons:** Monthly cost
- **Best for:** "Try it yourself" experience without signup

## Recording Tips for SaaS Demos

### Pre-Recording
- [ ] Clean up browser (no personal bookmarks visible)
- [ ] Use a fresh/demo account with good sample data
- [ ] Close notifications (Slack, email, etc.)
- [ ] Set resolution to 1920x1080 or 1280x720
- [ ] Use a clean desktop wallpaper

### During Recording
- [ ] Keep it under 60-90 seconds for landing page
- [ ] Show the "before" (problem) briefly
- [ ] Focus on the "aha moment" (paste ‚Üí generate ‚Üí results)
- [ ] End with clear CTA

### Post-Recording
- [ ] Trim dead air at start/end
- [ ] Speed up slow parts (1.5x-2x)
- [ ] Add subtle background music (optional)
- [ ] Export at 1080p, MP4 format

## Voxify Demo Script Outline

**Duration:** 45-60 seconds

1. **Hook (5s)**
   "Watch a customer call become marketing gold in 60 seconds."

2. **Show empty upload (3s)**
   Navigate to upload page

3. **Paste transcript (5s)**
   Copy/paste sample call transcript

4. **Select type & generate (5s)**
   Pick "Sales Call", click Generate

5. **Loading/processing (3s)**
   Show the cool loading animation

6. **Results reveal (20s)**
   - Scroll through headlines
   - Show email sequence
   - Quick look at social posts
   - Highlight a testimonial

7. **CTA (5s)**
   "Start free. No credit card required."
   Show "Try Free" button
