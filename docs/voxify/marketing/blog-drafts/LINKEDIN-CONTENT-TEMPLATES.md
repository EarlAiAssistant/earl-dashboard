## Pro Tips

1. **First line matters most** ‚Äî It's what shows before "see more." Make it hook.

2. **Use line breaks** ‚Äî Dense paragraphs get skipped. White space = readability.

3. **End with engagement prompt** ‚Äî Questions, requests for opinions, or CTAs drive comments.

4. **Link in comments, not post** ‚Äî LinkedIn deprioritizes posts with links in the body.

5. **Reply to every comment** ‚Äî First hour engagement signals quality to the algorithm.

6. **Don't sell every post** ‚Äî 4:1 ratio of value posts to promotional posts.

7. **Use your own experience** ‚Äî Templates are starting points. Personal details make them real.
