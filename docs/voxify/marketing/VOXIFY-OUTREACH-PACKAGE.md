## 3. Target List - People to Reach Out To

### Marketing Consultants/Freelancers on LinkedIn

Search: "marketing consultant" OR "freelance marketer" OR "content strategist"

**Specific profiles to find:**
1. People who post about customer research
2. People who mention case studies in their services
3. Fractional CMOs
4. Content marketing freelancers
5. B2B marketing consultants

**Look for signals:**
- "I help [companies] with content"
- "Customer-led growth"
- "Voice of customer"
- Posts about interviewing customers

### Agency Owners

Search: "marketing agency founder" OR "agency owner" OR "creative agency"

**Best targets:**
- Small agencies (5-20 people)
- B2B focused agencies
- Content/inbound marketing agencies
- Case study specialists

### Coaches & Consultants

Search: "business coach" OR "executive coach" OR "sales coach"

**Look for:**
- People who do 1:1 calls with clients
- People who post testimonials (they have the raw material)
- High-ticket coaches (they record calls)

### SaaS Founders

**Where to find them:**
- Indie Hackers (indiehackers.com/products)
- Product Hunt recent launches
- Twitter #buildinpublic
- LinkedIn "founder" + "SaaS"

**Best targets:**
- Early stage (need content, no marketing team)
- B2B SaaS (do customer calls)
- Solo founders or small teams

## Daily Outreach Goal

**Target: 10 outreach messages per day**

- 5 LinkedIn DMs
- 3 LinkedIn connection requests with notes
- 2 Twitter DMs or replies

**Time required:** ~30 min/day

**Expected response rate:** 10-20% (1-2 conversations per day)

---

## Quick Tips

1. **Personalize the first line** - Reference something specific about them
2. **Don't pitch immediately** - Ask a question first
3. **Keep it short** - Under 100 words for DMs
4. **Follow up once** - If no response after 5-7 days, one follow-up is fine
5. **Track everything** - Know what's working
