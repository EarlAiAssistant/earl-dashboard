- Easy out ("no pressure")

## Email Response Time Best Practices

### Interview Requests:
- **Send:** Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm (highest open rates)
- **Follow up:** After 3 days if no response (1 follow-up max)

### Testimonial Requests:
- **Send:** Within 24 hours of positive interaction (strike while iron is hot)
- **Follow up:** After 5 days if no response

### Case Study Permission:
- **Send:** 1-2 days after interview (while it's fresh in their mind)
- **Follow up:** After 1 week if no response

### Re-engagement:
- **Send:** After 14 days of inactivity
- **Follow up:** After 7 days (last chance email)

### Win-Back:
- **Send:** 14 days after cancellation
- **Follow up:** 30 days after cancellation (final email)
