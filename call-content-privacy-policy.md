## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Where We Store Your Data
- **Account data:** Stored on Supabase servers (US and EU regions available)
- **Transcripts and content:** Stored securely in encrypted databases
- **Payment data:** Stored by Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant)

### 5.2 How We Protect Your Data
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Regular security audits
- Access controls (role-based permissions)
- Two-factor authentication (available)

### 5.3 Data Retention
- **Active accounts:** We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Deleted accounts:** We delete your data within 30 days of account deletion
- **Backups:** Backup data is retained for 90 days for disaster recovery

**Exception:** We may retain certain data longer if required by law or to resolve disputes.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### 6.1 Access
You can access, download, and review your personal information at any time through your account dashboard.

### 6.2 Correction
You can update or correct your information through your account settings.

### 6.3 Deletion (Right to Be Forgotten)
You can request deletion of your account and data by:
- Deleting your account through settings
- Emailing us at privacy@call-content.com

We will delete your data within 30 days.

### 6.4 Data Portability
You can export your data (transcripts, generated content) at any time through your account dashboard.

### 6.5 Opt-Out of Marketing
You can unsubscribe from marketing emails by:
- Clicking "Unsubscribe" in any marketing email
- Updating your email preferences in account settings

### 6.6 Do Not Track
We currently do not respond to Do Not Track signals, but you can control cookies through your browser settings.
