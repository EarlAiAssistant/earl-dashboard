## Production Notes

### Visual Style:
- **Clean, minimal UI** (not cluttered)
- **Fast pacing** (cut dead air, keep it moving)
- **Use real product** (not mockupsâ€”show actual Call-Content interface)
- **Annotations** (highlight key buttons, features with arrows/circles)

### Voiceover:
- **Tone:** Conversational, helpful (not salesy)
- **Pacing:** 120-140 words/minute (slightly faster than normal speech = energetic)
- **Voice:** Professional but friendly (think: explaining to a colleague, not pitching a stranger)

### Music:
- **Background track:** Upbeat, modern (not cheesy corporate music)
- **Volume:** Low (30% maxâ€”voiceover is priority)
- **Suggestion:** "Inspiring Corporate" or "Tech Product" tracks from Epidemic Sound

### Screen Recording Tips:
1. **Use 1920x1080 resolution** (scales well for YouTube, social)
2. **Hide desktop clutter** (close unnecessary tabs, clean desktop)
3. **Use smooth scrolling** (no jerky mouse movements)
4. **Highlight cursor** (make it larger/more visible in recording)
5. **Zoom in on key actions** (upload button, generate button)

## Alternative: Loom / Async Video

If professional production is too much:

**Quick Version (15 minutes to create):**
1. Open Loom (or Zoom recording)
2. Record yourself talking through the product (2-3 minutes)
3. Show screen + your face in corner (builds trust)
4. Keep it casual ("Hey, let me show you how this works...")
5. Upload directly to website

**Pros:** Authentic, fast to create, feels personal  
**Cons:** Less polished, longer (3-5 min typical)
