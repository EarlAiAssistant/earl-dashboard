### **Day -6 to -4: Prepare Your Listing**

**Product Hunt Listing Requirements:**

1. **Tagline** (60 chars max)
   - One-sentence description
   - Example: "Turn customer calls into blog posts, case studies, and social posts"

2. **Description** (260 chars max)
   - Expand on the tagline
   - Example: "Upload a customer interview, get 12 ready-to-use marketing assets in 10 minutes. No more spending hours writing case studies from scratch."

3. **Thumbnail** (240x240px)
   - Your logo or product screenshot
   - High contrast, clean design
   - Example: Call-Content logo on blue background

4. **Gallery Images** (3-5 images, 1270x760px)
   - Screenshot 1: Dashboard (upload interface)
   - Screenshot 2: Generated content (blog post example)
   - Screenshot 3: Output examples (case study, social posts)
   - Screenshot 4: ROI (time saved, before/after)
   - Screenshot 5: Integrations (Zapier, WordPress, etc.)

5. **First Comment** (this is your launch post)
   - 300-500 words
   - Tell the story (problem ‚Üí solution ‚Üí how it works)
   - Include: CTA, link to demo, special offer

**Action:**
- [ ] Write tagline + description

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ‚ùå **Mistake #1: Launching on a Monday or Friday**
**Why it fails:** Low traffic, less visibility  
**Fix:** Launch Tuesday-Thursday

---

### ‚ùå **Mistake #2: Not Preparing Supporters**
**Why it fails:** No early momentum = algorithm buries you  
**Fix:** Line up 10-15 people to upvote in first hour

---

### ‚ùå **Mistake #3: Writing a Generic First Comment**
**Why it fails:** Doesn't hook readers, no social proof  
**Fix:** Tell a story (problem ‚Üí solution ‚Üí how it works)

---

### ‚ùå **Mistake #4: Ignoring Comments**
**Why it fails:** Looks like you don't care, reduces engagement  
**Fix:** Respond to every comment within 1-2 hours

---

### ‚ùå **Mistake #5: Not Offering a Special Deal**
**Why it fails:** No urgency to sign up  
**Fix:** "30% off for Product Hunt users" or "Lifetime deal for first 100"
