## 1. What is Call-Content?

**Call-Content turns your customer conversations into marketing content.**

Instead of spending hours writing blog posts, case studies, and social media posts from scratch, you:
1. **Record** a customer conversation (sales call, success check-in, interview)
2. **Upload** the transcript to Call-Content
3. **Generate** blog posts, case studies, testimonials, and social media content in minutes

**Perfect for:**
- SaaS founders and marketers
- Consultants and agencies
- Coaches and course creators
- Anyone who talks to customers regularly

<a name="uploading"></a>
## 4. Uploading Your First Transcript

### Where to Get Transcripts

**Option 1: Record and Transcribe with Zoom**
1. Enable **Cloud Recording** in Zoom
2. Enable **Audio Transcript** in Zoom settings
3. After your call, download the transcript from Zoom Cloud

**Option 2: Use a Transcription Tool**
- **Otter.ai** (free for 600 min/month)
- **Fireflies.ai** (free, auto-joins calls)
- **Rev.com** (99% accuracy, $1.50/minute)

**Option 3: Manual Transcription**
- If you have audio/video, upload it to one of the above tools
- Export the transcript as a `.txt` or `.srt` file

### Upload to Call-Content

1. Click **"New Project"** on your dashboard
2. Choose upload method:
   - **Upload File** (drag and drop or click to browse)
   - **Paste Text** (copy/paste the transcript directly)
   - **Import from URL** (if supported by your transcription tool)
3. Add project details:
   - **Project Name** (e.g., "Sarah Interview - Acme Corp")
   - **Customer Name** (optional, but helps with personalization)
   - **Date** (auto-filled)
4. Click **"Upload"**

**Supported formats:** `.txt`, `.doc`, `.docx`, `.srt`, `.vtt`, `.pdf`

<a name="generating-content"></a>
## 5. Generating Content

Once your transcript is uploaded, you can generate different types of content:

### Choose a Template

**Available Templates:**
1. **Blog Post** – Long-form article (1,500-2,500 words)
2. **Case Study** – Problem → Solution → Results format
3. **Social Media Posts** – 5-10 posts for LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.
4. **Testimonial Quotes** – 3-5 short quotes (1-2 sentences each)
5. **Email Newsletter** – Summary with key insights
6. **FAQ Content** – Common questions from the conversation
7. **Sales Script** – How you handled objections
8. **Product Feedback** – Feature requests and pain points

### Generate Content

1. Select your transcript
2. Click **"Generate Content"**
3. Choose a template (or select multiple)
4. Click **"Generate"**
5. Wait 10-30 seconds (depending on transcript length)

**What happens:**
- Call-Content analyzes the transcript
- Identifies key themes, quotes, and insights
- Generates content using AI
- Presents a draft for you to review

---

### Edit and Refine

**The AI-generated content is 80-90% done.** Here's what you should do:

**Review and edit:**
- ✅ Check for accuracy (did the AI get the facts right?)
- ✅ Add your brand voice (make it sound like you)
- ✅ Remove filler words (um, uh, repetition)
- ✅ Add context (industry-specific details)
- ✅ Optimize for SEO (title tags, headers, keywords)

**Pro Tip:** The AI is great at structure and speed, but you should always add the human touch.

<a name="organizing"></a>
## 7. Organizing Your Content

### Projects and Folders

**Projects:**
- Each transcript upload creates a "Project"
- Projects contain all generated content from that transcript
- Organize by customer, date, or topic

**How to organize:**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Projects**
2. Use **Tags** (e.g., "Case Study", "Customer Success", "Sales")
3. Use **Search** to find specific projects
4. **Archive** old projects to keep your dashboard clean

---

### Collaboration (Team Plans)

**Invite team members:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Click **"Invite Member"**
3. Enter their email
4. Assign role (Admin, Editor, Viewer)

**Roles:**
- **Admin:** Full access (billing, settings, invites)
- **Editor:** Can create, edit, and delete projects
- **Viewer:** Can view but not edit

## 10. Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: What is Call-Content?**  
A: Call-Content turns customer conversations (transcripts) into marketing content (blog posts, case studies, social media posts).

**Q: Who is Call-Content for?**  
A: SaaS founders, marketers, consultants, coaches, agencies — anyone who talks to customers and needs content.

**Q: Do I need to record calls myself?**  
A: Yes. Call-Content processes transcripts, not audio. Use Zoom, Otter.ai, or Fireflies to record and transcribe calls.

---

### Pricing & Billing

**Q: Is there a free trial?**  
A: Yes! 14 days, no credit card required.

**Q: What happens after my trial ends?**  
A: You can still access your content, but you can't generate new content until you subscribe.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**  
A: Yes. Cancel through Settings → Billing. You retain access until the end of your paid period.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**  
A: Yes, within the first 14 days of a paid subscription (full refund).

---

### Features & Usage

**Q: What file formats do you accept?**  
A: `.txt`, `.doc`, `.docx`, `.srt`, `.vtt`, `.pdf`

**Q: How long does content generation take?**  
A: 10-30 seconds, depending on transcript length.

**Q: Can I edit generated content?**  
A: Yes! The AI gives you an 80-90% complete draft. You should always review and edit for accuracy and voice.

**Q: Can I export content?**  
A: Yes. Export as Markdown, Word, HTML, Plain Text, or PDF.

**Q: Is there a limit to transcript length?**  
A: Recommended: 30-60 minutes of conversation (5,000-10,000 words). Longer transcripts may take longer to process.

---

### Data & Privacy

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes. We use encryption (HTTPS, AES-256) and store data on secure servers (Supabase). Read our [Privacy Policy](https://call-content.com/privacy).

**Q: Do you sell my data?**  
A: No. Never.

**Q: Who can see my transcripts?**  
A: Only you (and team members you invite). We do not share your data publicly.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**  
A: We delete your data within 30 days of account deletion (unless required by law).

**Q: Can I delete my account?**  
A: Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.

---

### Technical

**Q: Do you integrate with Zoom/Otter/Fireflies?**  
A: Not yet, but it's on our roadmap. For now, you can download transcripts and upload them manually.

**Q: Can I use the API?**  
A: Not yet. API access is planned for future releases.

**Q: Do you have a mobile app?**  
A: Not yet. The web app is mobile-friendly, but we're considering a native app in the future.
